Protein & Your Meds

Protein and Parkinson's meds don't always play nicely together. I use vegan protein powder — no issues! If you use animal protein, take your pill 2 hours away from that meal.

💪

Why Protein Matters

As we age, protein becomes essential — for muscle mass, bone health, immune function, hormone regulation, and fighting sarcopenia. Don't skip it!

For the Ladies

Women — This One's for You 👩

Protein isn't just for men. I actually think it's even MORE important for women.

Ladies, Listen Up! 😉

After 45, hormonal shifts from menopause can accelerate muscle loss and weaken bones. Protein fights back — supporting muscle retention, metabolic function, weight management, and bone density. It's not optional, it's essential. Make it a daily priority and your future self will thank you.

💊

Quick Reminder About Your Meds

Vegan protein powder (like the one I use from Costco) is totally fine with your Parkinson's medication — no waiting needed! If you do use animal protein (eggs, chicken, fish), try to take your levodopa at least 2 hours before or after that meal whenever possible.

High-Protein Power Waffles
Preteen-approved — my whole family of 4 loves these!
High Protein Dairy-Free GF Option
Ingredients
  • 2 scoops vegan protein powder (I use Costco's)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 2 tbsp nut butter
  • 1½ cups rolled oats (I use Costco GF oats — regular works too!)
  • 1 ripe banana
  • Optional: handful of chocolate chips 🍫
Method
Blend everything together. Spray your waffle maker generously with coconut oil — don't be shy or they'll stick! Pour in the batter and cook 3–4 minutes until golden. That's it. Seriously.
💪 ~25–30g protein per serving

MIND Diet Berry Protein Smoothie
Blueberries are a MIND diet superstar — packed with brain-protective antioxidants
High Protein MIND Diet Dairy-Free 5 Min
Ingredients
  • 1 scoop vegan protein powder
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries (or mixed berries)
  • 1 cup almond or oat milk
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
  • ½ frozen banana
  • Handful of spinach (you won't taste it!)
Method
Throw everything in the blender and blitz until smooth. Drink immediately or pour into a jar and refrigerate overnight for a grab-and-go breakfast.
💪 ~22g protein per serving

Easy Red Lentil & Spinach Soup
Make a big pot on Sunday — eat all week. Legumes are a MIND diet must!
High Protein MIND Diet Vegan
Ingredients
  • 1½ cups red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 big handfuls of fresh spinach
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tsp cumin + 1 tsp turmeric
  • Olive oil, salt & pepper
  • Squeeze of lemon to finish
Method
Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil for 3 minutes. Add spices, then lentils, tomatoes, and broth. Simmer 20 minutes until lentils are soft. Stir in spinach and lemon juice. Done! Freezes beautifully.
💪 ~18g protein per bowl

10-Minute Salmon Power Bowl
Fatty fish = top MIND diet food. Omega-3s for your brain, protein for your muscles
High Protein MIND Diet 10 Min
Ingredients
  • 1 salmon fillet (fresh or canned wild salmon works great!)
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice or quinoa
  • Big handful of leafy greens or kale
  • ½ avocado, sliced
  • Handful of cherry tomatoes
  • Drizzle of olive oil + squeeze of lemon
  • Salt, pepper, and everything bagel seasoning
Method
Pan-sear or bake the salmon 8–10 minutes. Assemble your bowl: grains first, then greens, salmon, avocado, and tomatoes. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon. Done in the time it takes to cook the rice!
💪 ~40g protein per bowl

Loaded Chickpea & Walnut Salad
Walnuts are a MIND diet superstar — eat them daily if you can!
High Protein MIND Diet Vegan No Cook
Ingredients
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • ¼ cup walnuts, roughly chopped
  • 2 cups mixed greens or arugula
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ¼ red onion, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup parsley or fresh herbs
  • 3 tbsp olive oil + 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard + salt & pepper
Method
Whisk together the olive oil, lemon, and mustard for the dressing. Toss everything else together in a big bowl, drizzle over the dressing, and toss again. Zero cooking required — done in 5 minutes flat.
💪 ~18g protein per serving

Overnight Protein Oats
Make it tonight, eat it tomorrow. Breakfast done before you wake up!
High Protein MIND Diet Dairy-Free 5 Min Prep
Ingredients
  • ½ cup rolled oats (GF if needed)
  • 1 scoop vegan protein powder
  • ¾ cup almond or oat milk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • ½ cup blueberries or strawberries (fresh or frozen)
  • Drizzle of maple syrup or honey to taste
Method
Stir oats, protein powder, milk, chia seeds, and almond butter together in a jar. Pop the lid on and refrigerate overnight. In the morning, top with berries and a little sweetener. Grab and go!
💪 ~25g protein per jar
