Recipe 01 · Gut Health · Immunity

Instant Pot Chicken Bone Broth

A mineral-rich tonic that boosts your immune system, improves digestion, and is rich in collagen and glucosamine — helping ease achy joints, rehydrate skin, and build stronger bones. Homemade is always best.

⏱ ~3 hours total
🍗 1 whole chicken carcass
❄️ Freezes beautifully
🫙 Store 7 days fridge

🍗 The Bones

  • Bones, skin, cartilage from 1 roast chicken
  • Gizzards, neck, any extras
  • Chicken feet if available (from your butcher)

🥕 The Extras

  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Herbs: rosemary, thyme, sage, bay leaves
  • High-quality sea salt & pepper
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar (helps break down bones)
  • Filtered water to just cover

📋 Quick Method

  1. 1Add bones + veggies + herbs + salt + 1 tbsp ACV to Instant Pot. Cover with cold filtered water (1 inch over bones — no more).
  2. 2Select "Soup" setting → manually set to 119 minutes (or High Pressure, 120 min).
  3. 3Let pressure release naturally (~20 min). Or use quick release if in a hurry.
  4. 4Strain through fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth into a heat-safe glass pitcher. Push on bones to extract all broth.
  5. 5Skim fat cap (or refrigerate first and remove solidified fat cap). Store in mason jars — leave 1 inch space if freezing!

Christine's tip: I cook my broth for 2 hours minimum — 30 minutes creates chicken broth, not the rich, thick, might-even-gel-in-the-fridge bone broth you want. The more time, the better!

Recipe 02 · Anti-Inflammatory · Brain Health

Anti-Inflammatory Golden Milk

Curcumin — turmeric's most powerful compound — has medicinal properties used to reduce joint pain, arthritis, inflammation, and even treat depression. This warm drink is your daily dose of golden goodness.

⏱ 20 minutes
☕ 1 serving
🌿 Dairy-free
Reduces inflammation
Joint pain relief
Improves digestion
Supports liver health
Eases IBS symptoms
Lifts mood
Reduces bloating
Lowers cholesterol

🧴 Ingredients

  • 1 cup organic full-fat coconut milk (BPA-free can) — OR — 1 cup unsweetened almond milk + 1 tsp coconut oil
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • Big pinch freshly ground black pepper (activates curcumin!)
  • 1 small piece fresh ginger root (¼ inch, peeled & grated)
  • 1 big pinch ground cardamom
  • ¼ tsp raw honey — added off the heat (optional)

📋 Instructions

  1. 1Blend or whisk all ingredients together.
  2. 2Warm over medium heat for 15 minutes. Let it gently simmer — do not boil.
  3. 3For a frothier drink, blend in a high-speed blender before serving.
  4. 4Add honey off the heat to preserve its enzymes.

Why black pepper? The piperine in black pepper increases curcumin absorption by up to 2000%. Never skip it! Even a couple of grinds makes a huge difference.

Recipe 03 · Immunity · Energy · Anti-inflammatory

Immune Boosting Wellness Smoothie

Packed with fresh ginger, turmeric root, spinach, pineapple, and banana — this smoothie is absolutely delicious, great for digestion, and a daily boost for your immune system. Christine drinks this every day!

⏱ 5 minutes
🥤 Makes 2 smoothies
🌱 Plant-based
~139 cal each

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup frozen pineapple
  • 2–3 cups organic spinach
  • 1 knob fresh ginger, peeled (~2 inches)
  • 1 knob fresh turmeric root (~2 inches)
  • ½ tbsp manuka or raw honey
  • ⅛ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • ½–¾ cup almond milk or coconut milk

📋 Instructions

  1. 1Add all ingredients to a large high-powered blender.
  2. 2Blend on high for 1–2 minutes until completely smooth.
  3. 3Add more almond milk if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  4. 4Pour and enjoy — makes 2 smoothies!

Swaps & Add-ons: Add a scoop of collagen protein powder — it has no taste and adds a great protein boost! No bananas? Sub a cored apple instead.
